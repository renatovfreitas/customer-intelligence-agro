# Business Questions

## Main Question

Who are the most valuable customers for the company?

## Customer Questions

- Which customers generated the most revenue in the last 12 months?
- Which customers generated the most profit?
- Which customers bought most frequently?
- Which customers have not purchased recently?
- Which customers buy from multiple product groups?
- Which customers bought only once?

## Product Questions

- Which product groups generate the most revenue?
- Which product groups generate the highest profit?
- Which products sell a lot but have low margin?
- Are there duplicate products registered with different IDs?

## Segmentation Questions

- Who are the strategic customers?
- Who are occasional customers?
- Who are inactive customers?
- Who are high-potential customers?
